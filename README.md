## ‚ú® Features 
- Can track any spell (Fireball) or proc (Shadow Trance).
- Alerts about incoming spells at CAST START to give you time to react.
- Very customizable.
- You can add your own spells and procs.

![Druid](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6a8e7006-2921-47f5-9f63-a9ce30b7b6f8)

## ‚ú® Tracking Options

- Track on Player ‚û°Ô∏è Tracks spells targeting the Player.
- Track by Player ‚û°Ô∏è Tracks spells cast by the Player, including self-procs.
- Track on Target ‚û°Ô∏è Tracks spells targeting your current Target.
- Track by Target ‚û°Ô∏è Tracks spells cast by your current Target.
- Track on Friendly ‚û°Ô∏è Tracks spells targeting friendly units (including the Player).
- Track by Friendly ‚û°Ô∏è Tracks spells cast by friendly units other than the Player.
- Track on Hostile ‚û°Ô∏è Tracks spells targeting hostile units.
- Track by Hostile ‚û°Ô∏è Tracks spells cast by hostile units.
